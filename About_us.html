About Us

UniLocker is an innovative mobile application designed to streamline the college application process by connecting students with the universities. The app offers a centralised platform where students can apply to multiple universities and manage their application process from start to finish. UniLocker allows universities to receive applications and communicate with students seamlessly, while the government can monitor and regulate the process. By offering all the application processes in one place, UniLocker simplifies the application process, saving time and reducing the chances of errors or delays. UniLocker also provides students with access to information about various universities, scholarships, and other resources.
